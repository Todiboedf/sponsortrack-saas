# Sponsorlens — Investment & Roadmap

*One-pager prepared for the Group 1 discussion — June 2026*

---

## What Sponsorlens is

The **sponsor-intelligence layer for sport**: it measures, proves and grows the value of every sponsorship — across social, in-venue and broadcast — and turns it into the reports that renew contracts.

**Why it's relevant to Group 1.** Group 1 sells, activates and monetises sponsorship inventory for rights holders and brands — but has no measurement / ROI product. Sponsorlens is exactly that missing layer: the proof engine behind Group 1's commercial pitch and renewals.

---

## The honest state of the technology

- **Social & owned-media analytics — live today.** Automated ingestion + KPI/EMV computation already running (Supabase pipeline, daily collection).
- **Computer-vision logo measurement — the build ahead.** Detecting sponsor exposure (jersey, LED, on-screen time) on match footage. The ML is a solved class of problem (YOLO-grade models); **the real constraint is footage access, not the algorithm.**

### Footage access, by tier (this is the moat — handled honestly)

| Tier | Source | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Owned & social** (club/player content) | Clean, available now → where the POC runs |
| 2 | **In-venue** (stadium LED/signage, club-authorised capture) | Club can grant |
| 3 | **Broadcast monitoring** (recorded-broadcast analysis, media-monitoring model — Nielsen Sports lineage) | Grey-zone at POC, formalised with licences at scale |
| 4 | **Official league/broadcaster partnership** (real-time + audited € media value, multi-league) | Premium tier — **this is where Group 1's rights-holder relationships unlock access** |

## Roadmap & investment — 3 levels

**Level 0 · Proof of concept — now.**
One real match, 2–3 sponsor logos, measured screen-time → a sample exposure report.
**Cost: ~€0–500 + founder time.** Purpose: de-risk and demo.

**Level 1 · Sellable MVP.**
Productised CV pipeline + the existing dashboard + first paying property (Osasuna).

| Item | Detail | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| **Team** | 1 CV/ML engineer (key hire) + 1 product engineer; founder on product & commercial; annotation outsourced | 70–90% of budget |
| **Compute** | Cloud GPU for video processing, scales with match volume | hundreds €/mo early |
| **Data** | Audience data to convert exposure into € media value (partner, or public estimates to start) | variable |
| **Legal** | GDPR + rights/licence formalisation | one-off |

**12-month runway:** ~**€50–100k** ultra-lean (founder-led + freelancers) → ~**€200–400k** with a small full-time team. Salary-dominated.

**Level 2 · Scale.**
Multi-property, multi-league, official partnerships, real-time. The capital-intensive tier — peers raised **$2.6M (Blinkfire) → $39M (SponsorUnited) → $75M (Relo Metrics)** at this stage.
